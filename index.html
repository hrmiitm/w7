Financial Calculations

Return on Equity (ROE)

$$ROE = \frac{Net\ Income}{Shareholders'\ Equity} \times 100$$

Price-to-Earnings Ratio

$$P/E = \frac{Market\ Price\ per\ Share}{Earnings\ per\ Share}$$

Compound Annual Growth Rate

$$CAGR = \left(\frac{Ending\ Value}{Beginning\ Value}\right)^{\frac{1}{years}} - 1$$

These formulas are fundamental to our financial analysis. Our current ROE of 12.1% indicates efficient use of shareholder equity. The P/E ratio helps investors understand our market valuation relative to earnings.
